Open When You Want to Feel Loved

Jab tumhe kabhi lage ke tumhe pyaar chahiye, thoda sa apnapan, toh bas aankhein band kar lena… aur mehsoos karna mera pyaar tumhare aas-paas. Main chahti hoon ke tum kabhi akela mehsoos na karo, kyunki mera dil hamesha tumhare liye dhadakta hai. Chahe kitni bhi doori ho, meri feelings kabhi kam nahi hoti. Jab bhi tumhe pyaar ki zarurat ho, yaad rakhna — tum kisi ke liye bahut khaas ho… mere liye.❤️
