Open When You're Angry

Jab tum gussa ho jaate ho, toh main jaanti hoon ke andar kuch gehra dard chhupa hota hai. Gussa ek tarah ka aansu hota hai jo shabdon mein nahi nikal pata. Main chahti hoon ke tum apna gussa mujhe dikhao, chupane ki zarurat nahi. Main yahan hoon, tumhare saath — bina judge kiye, bina dare. Aur jab tumhara gussa thoda kam ho, toh mujhe apni baaton se woh muskaan laane dena jo tumhari rooh ko sukoon de. ❤️
