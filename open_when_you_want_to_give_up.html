Open When You Want to Give Up

Jab tumhe lage ke sab kuch itna mushkil ho gaya hai ke ab aage badhna mushkil hai, toh ruk jao aur ek baar meri baat suno. Har andhere ke baad roshni hoti hai, aur tumhari zindagi mein bhi wo roshni zaroor aayegi. Main jaanti hoon tum thak gaye ho, par tum itne strong ho ki haar maan lena tumhare liye sahi nahi. Tumhari har koshish ka matlab hai, har dard ka ek sabab hai. Main tumhare saath hoon, hamesha, chahe kuch bhi ho jaaye. Give up mat karna, kyunki tumhari kahani abhi baaki hai. 🛤️💓
